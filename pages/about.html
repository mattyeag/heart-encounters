About Heart Encounters

A Creative Arts & Equipping Center

We are a community of artisans, teachers, neighbors and friends who engage our community through music, the arts, theater and training that equips for living an abundant life.


In all our classes, activities and events, we endeavor to demonstrate the wonders of God through expressions of creativity and beauty. We host a worshipful atmosphere that cultivates the creation of true masterpieces and changed lives that impact the community by focusing on things that matter - things that unite rather than divide, things that bring purpose, value and substance to life.

Our Story

Tyrone Elementary School experienced its first remodel when Compass Dance Academy moved into the front of the the school. Walking down the halls of Compass, you can see adults and children alike, dancing and filling the building with laughter and joy as they experience the wonder of dance and movement. Who could have imagined Tyrone Elementary would have yet another transformation?


One morning late in May 2022, leaders from Compass along with artists, singers and teachers met to consider the unused part of Tyrone Elementary. To our delight and surprise, the Fayette County Board of Education made the back half of Tyrone Elementary available to artisans, singers, performers and teachers. A creative arts and equipping center called Heart Encounters was born.
